The Reminder Bot Tutorial

Check that your TimeZone is set to your correcet time zone within Twitter.





Follow @thereminderbot (it will not work unless you follow!).




Send a direct message to @thereminderbot with the following syntax:
<hour> : <minute> : <AM/PM> : <month number> / <day number> : <message>
The hour, minute, AM/PM, month, and day number specify when you get your reminder. The reminder that will be sent is contained in the message section.
Examples:
11:55 : PM : 11/21 : Wake up!!
11:15 : AM : 11/26 : Lunch with Greg
Note: don't forget to type the colons in!




Check the log to see if any errors.




Take a deep breath and clear your conscious. We will send your reminder at the specified time.
